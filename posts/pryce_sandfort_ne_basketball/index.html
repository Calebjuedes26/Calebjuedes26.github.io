Pryce Sandfort was the best shooter in the Big ten

huskers
basketball
shooters
Author

Caleb Juedes

Published

April 10, 2026

This year for Nebraska men basketball fans was a year to remember. Coming into the year fans were optimistic as always but this year was different. The huskers were projected towards the bottom half of the big ten coming into the year.

The Nebraska Cornhuskers were looking to build off the momentum the team had captured at the end of the previous season. Nebraska would be led by incoming transfers of likes such as Jamarques Lawerence returning back home and newcomer Pryce Sandfort.

Anyone watching the Nebraska mens seasson saw a completed different team. Pryce Sandfort lead the Cornhuskers to a historic year and made the schools first ever sweet 16 appearance. Its not a crazy statement to say that Pryce Sandfort was one of the most valuable players in college basketball this season.

We are going to look at some data and have some visuals to back up the argument.The data we will be looking at will all have came from the same place. Sports Reference college basketball is the site I used. Sports Reference had multiple fields to choose from. Throughout this blog post I will be looking at Sandfort against this college teammates and then look at a bigger picture such as how he faired compared to the rest of the Big Ten.

The first visualization we will look at is going to be some bar charts comparing to Pryce Sandfort stats to the rest of the Nebraska Cornhuskers. You can look at any field you are wanting to look at but for this post since I am telling people he is the best shooter in college basketball, I will need to look at shooting metrics to see these results. Looking at the charts you can tell that Pryce Sandfort lead the team in almost all of the offensive categories. According to the charts we can see that Pryce has a sizable lead on the next closest husker.

The Big Ten Conference is one of the hardest conferences to get wins in night in and night out and the Huskers were able to capture that energy most nights. To beat good teams in the Big Ten you most play good also. Lets take a look at a few visualization that show how Pryce Sandfort stacks amongst other Big Ten foes.
